Featured Recipe: Yam and Scrambled egg

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Yam 2-3 Medium sized
Eggs 4-6
Salt 1/2 tea spoon
Vegetable Oil 1-2 cups
Crayfish and Veggies for extra flavor As mush as you want (Optional)

Preparation Steps

1. Peel and boil yam cubes until tender (15-20 minutes).

2. Whisk eggs with salt, heat oil in a pan, and scramble eggs (with crayfish and veggies) until cooked.

3. Serve scrambled eggs alongside boiled yam.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Cabbage As much as you like
Carrot As much as you like
Lettuce Leaves As much as you like
Green Beans As much as you like
Peas As much as you like
Cucumber 1-2
Plantain 2-3
Vegetable Oil 1-2 cups

Preparation Steps

Salad

1. Chop veggies (cabbage, carrot, lettuce leaves, green beans, peas, cucumbers).

2. Mix and add dressing.

3. Serve chilled.

Plantain

1. Peel and slice plantains.

2. Fry in hot oil until golden brown.

3. Drain and serve hot.

Featured Recipe: Chocolate Souffle

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Butter (for greasing the ramekins) 2 Tablespoons
Granulated sugar (for coating the ramekins) 1/4 cup
Dark Chocolate (chopped) 4 oz (115g)
Unsalted Butter 2 tablespoons
Vanilla Extract 1 Tablespoon
Large Egg (separate yolks and whites) 3
Granulated sugar (for the egg whites) 1/4 cup
Salt Pinch

Preparation Steps

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease 4 ramekins with butter and dust with sugar. Set aside.

2. Melt 4 oz chocolate and 2 tbsp butter together (double boiler or microwave). Stir in 1 tsp vanilla extract. Let cool slightly.

3. Beat 3 egg yolks and 1/4 cup sugar until light and creamy. Add the melted chocolate mixture and combine.

4. Beat 3 egg whites with a pinch of salt until soft peaks form. Gradually add 1/4 cup sugar and beat until stiff peaks form.

5. Gently fold the whipped egg whites into the chocolate mixture in batches until well combined.

6. Spoon the mixture into ramekins, smoothing the tops and running a finger around the edges.

7. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the soufflés rise and the tops are golden. The center should remain soft.

8. Dust with powdered sugar and serve while warm. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
