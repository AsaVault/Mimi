Featured Recipe: Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Arborio Rice 1.5 cups
Mixed Mushrooms 400g
White Wine 1/2 cup
Vegetable Stock 4 cups
Parmesan Cheese 1 cup

Preparation Steps

1. In a large pan, sauté mushrooms until golden brown. Set aside.

2. In the same pan, toast the rice until translucent.

3. Add wine and stir until absorbed.

4. Gradually add hot stock, stirring constantly until rice is creamy and al dente.

5. Fold in mushrooms and Parmesan. Season to taste.
